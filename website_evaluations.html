Website Evaluations

Apple

Apple's website is an excellent example of modern web design. It follows the CRAP principles by creating a clean, organized, and visually appealing experience.

  • Contrast: Strong contrast between text, images, and background makes information easy to read.
  • Repetition: Consistent fonts, colors, buttons, and spacing create a cohesive appearance throughout the site.
  • Alignment: Every element is neatly aligned, making the page feel balanced and professional.
  • Proximity: Related content is grouped together, helping users quickly find information.

Arngren.net

Arngren.net is well known as an example of poor web design because it ignores many basic design principles.

  • Contrast: Too many colors and font styles compete for attention, making the page difficult to read.
  • Repetition: There is little consistency in fonts, image sizes, or layout.
  • Alignment: Images and text appear scattered across the page with no clear organization.
  • Proximity: Similar items are not grouped together, making it difficult to locate related information.
